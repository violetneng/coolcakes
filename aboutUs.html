about us

Cool Cakes is a small local cake shop located in San Jose, California. We offer high quality PNGs of cakes made by a friend and taken off the internet for prices you won't find anywhere else.

Our cakes are always made with the highest quality pixels, and each order is created with great care. We promise you'll be satisfied with the cake that you may or may not get.
